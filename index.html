Welcome to RealitY

RealitY is a modern web application that provides secure, personalized access to real-time insights using Google authentication. This page explains the app's purpose and how we handle your data.

What is RealitY?

RealitY is a secure and user-friendly platform that helps users gain access to real-time, personalized features and data insights. It integrates with Google Sign-In to streamline your experience while ensuring your privacy.

Why Google Login?

We use Google Sign-In to verify your identity, so you don't need to remember another password. We only request your basic profile info (name, email, and profile image). No data is sold or shared with third parties.

What Data Do We Collect?

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile image. This information is used solely to personalize your experience within RealitY. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any third-party purposes.

Who Should Use RealitY?

Anyone looking for a secure, customized experience that adapts to their profile and preferences. Ideal for individuals and developers seeking real-time dashboards or personalized web tools.
